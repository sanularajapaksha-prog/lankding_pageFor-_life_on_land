Biodiversity

Biodiversity is the variety of life on Earth — from the smallest microbes to the largest mammals. Preserving biodiversity is vital for ecosystem stability and human well-being.

Why Biodiversity Matters

Biodiversity supports ecosystem services like pollination, clean water, and carbon storage. It also offers genetic resources vital for agriculture and medicine.
